## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this audit is to evaluate the adequacy and effectiveness of controls within the defined audit area. The audit will assess whether controls are properly designed, implemented, and operating effectively to mitigate identified risks.

---

## 2. Objectives

The objectives of this audit are to:

* Evaluate the design and effectiveness of key controls
* Identify control gaps and deficiencies
* Assess compliance with internal policies and standards
* Validate risk mitigation measures
* Provide actionable recommendations for improvement

---

## 3. Scope

The scope of this audit includes:

* Systems, applications, and infrastructure within the selected audit area
* Relevant processes and control activities
* Associated data flows and integrations

**In-Scope Examples:**

* [e.g., Active Directory, Azure AD, IAM tools]

**Out-of-Scope Examples:**

* Systems not related to the defined audit area

## 5. Audit Criteria

## 6. Methodology

The audit will follow the established IT Audit Methodology, including:

* Planning and risk assessment
* Control identification and mapping
* Control testing (design and effectiveness)
* Evidence collection and validation
* Reporting and follow-up

---

## 7. Key Risks

The audit will focus on risks such as:

* Unauthorized access to systems and data
* Inadequate access controls or privilege management
* Lack of monitoring or logging
* Weak configuration or security controls
* Non-compliance with policies or standards

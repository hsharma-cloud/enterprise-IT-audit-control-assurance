## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to clearly define the boundaries of the audit engagement, including systems, processes, and controls that are included and excluded from the audit.

---

## 2. Scope Definition

This audit will evaluate controls within the defined audit area, focusing on systems, processes, and activities that support the control environment.

---

## 3. In-Scope Areas

The following components are included in the audit:

### 3.1 Systems

* [e.g., Active Directory, Azure AD, IAM platforms]

### 3.2 Processes

* User provisioning and deprovisioning
* Access reviews and certifications
* Privileged access management

### 3.3 Controls

* Authentication and authorization controls
* Role-based access control (RBAC)
* Logging and monitoring of access activities

### 3.4 Data

* User account data
* Access logs and audit trails
* Identity-related configuration data

---

## 4. Out-of-Scope Areas

The following are explicitly excluded from this audit:

* Systems not related to the defined audit area
* Physical security controls
* Non-IT business processes
* Third-party systems outside organizational control (unless specified)

---

## 5. Boundaries

### 5.1 Organizational Boundaries

* Applies to internal IT systems and environments

### 5.2 Technical Boundaries

* Includes production environments
* May include non-production environments if relevant to control testing

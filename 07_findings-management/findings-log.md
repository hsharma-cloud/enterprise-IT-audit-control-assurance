# Findings Log

**Project:** enterprise-IT-audit-control-assurance
**Version:** 1.0
**Owner:** IT Audit Function

---

## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to record identified audit findings, including control deficiencies, associated risks, and recommended remediation actions. It serves as the central repository for tracking issues identified during the audit.

---

## 2. Objectives

* Document control deficiencies identified during testing
* Assess the risk and impact of findings
* Provide actionable remediation recommendations
* Track ownership and status of findings

---

## 3. Findings Structure

Each finding includes:

* Finding ID
* Title
* Description
* Affected Control
* Risk Level
* Impact
* Root Cause
* Recommendation
* Owner
* Status

---

## 5. Risk Classification

### High Risk

* Significant impact on business operations or security
* Requires immediate remediation

### Medium Risk

* Moderate impact
* Requires timely remediation

### Low Risk

* Minor impact
* Recommended improvements

---

## 6. Root Cause Analysis

Each finding must include a root cause to ensure effective remediation. Common root causes include:

* Process gaps
* Lack of automation
* Inadequate monitoring
* Weak control design

---

## 7. Recommendations

Recommendations must be:

* Specific and actionable
* Aligned with control objectives
* Focused on reducing risk

---

## 8. Ownership and Accountability

Each finding must have an assigned owner responsible for:

* Implementing remediation
* Providing status updates
* Ensuring closure

---

## 9. Status Tracking

Findings are tracked using the following statuses:

* Open
* In Progress
* Remediated
* Closed

---

## 10. Integration with Audit Reporting

Findings documented in this log are used to:

* Develop the audit report
* Communicate issues to management
* Track remediation progress

---

## 11. Conclusion

The Findings Log provides a structured approach to documenting and managing audit issues, ensuring that risks are addressed and controls are improved.

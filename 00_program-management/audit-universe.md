# Audit Universe

**Project:** enterprise-IT-audit-control-assurance
**Version:** 1.0
**Owner:** IT Audit Function

---

## 1. Purpose

The Audit Universe defines the complete inventory of auditable entities within the enterprise. It provides a structured view of systems, processes, and functions subject to audit and serves as the foundation for risk-based audit planning.

---

## 2. Objectives

* Identify all auditable areas within the IT environment
* Establish a comprehensive inventory of systems and processes
* Enable risk-based prioritization of audits
* Ensure complete audit coverage over time

---

## 3. Scope

The Audit Universe includes all IT-related domains, including:

* Infrastructure (servers, storage, virtualization)
* Network and security systems
* Identity and access management
* Applications and databases
* Cloud and hybrid environments
* Security operations and monitoring
* Data protection and privacy
* Change and configuration management
* Business continuity and disaster recovery

---

## 4. Audit Universe Categories

### 4.1 Infrastructure

* Servers and operating systems
* Virtualization platforms
* Storage systems

### 4.2 Network Environment

* Firewalls and network segmentation
* VPN and remote access
* Routing and switching

### 4.3 Identity & Access Management

* User provisioning and deprovisioning
* Authentication mechanisms (MFA, SSO)
* Privileged access

### 4.4 Applications & Databases

* Business applications
* Web applications and APIs
* Database systems

### 4.5 Cloud & Hybrid

* Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure)
* Identity federation
* Cloud security configurations

### 4.6 Security Operations

* SIEM and monitoring
* Incident detection and response
* Logging systems

### 4.7 Vulnerability Management

* Vulnerability scanning
* Patch management
* Remediation tracking

### 4.8 Data Protection

* Data classification
* Encryption
* Data loss prevention

### 4.9 Change Management

* Change approvals
* Configuration baselines
* Deployment processes

### 4.10 Business Continuity

* Backup and recovery
* Disaster recovery planning
* Resilience strategies

---

## 5. Risk Classification

Each entity is classified based on risk:

* **High Risk:** Critical systems with high business impact
* **Medium Risk:** Important systems with moderate impact
* **Low Risk:** Supporting systems with limited impact

---

## 6. Maintenance

The Audit Universe must be:

* Reviewed annually
* Updated for new or decommissioned systems
* Aligned with enterprise architecture

---

## 7. Conclusion

The Audit Universe provides a comprehensive inventory of auditable entities and enables effective risk-based audit planning across the enterprise.
